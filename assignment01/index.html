Dimensions

The car is 4574mm Long, 1876mm Wide and 1664mm High with a 2720mm Wheelbase. Meanwhile, the vehicle has a Fuel Tank capacity of 37 liters.

Engine and Transmission

The car comes with a 1.5 Turbocharged GDi engine with an electric motor. The 1490cc displacement produces 160hp and 250Nm of Torque. Meanwhile, the electric motor produces 120hp and 230Nm of Torque. On the side, you will see 18-inch Alloy Wheels. Furthermore, the engine is mated with 10-Speed Automatic Transmission.

Exterior

At the front, the car has Projector LED Headlights, LED DRLs and Halogen Fog Lamps. Meanwhile, there are Sequential F & R Turning Lights. At the rear, you will see LED Rear Lights,

Interior

The first thing you will experience is Electric Power Steering (EPS) and Panoramic Sunroof, making the interior bright. Then there are 10-inch Touchscreen Infotainment, Dual Zone Auto AC with Rear Vents, Push Start, Power Tailgate and Smart Key. The options show that the car is filled with multiple latest tech and facilities. The Driver Seat is 6-Way Electrically Adjustable, making the drive experience very comfortable and convenient.

Safety

The car has a lot of safety features, including ABS + EBD, Traction Control System (TCS), Hill Start Assist (HSA) and Hill Descent Control (HDC). The other safety features are Cruise Control, Tyre Pressure Monitoring System (TPMS), Front Collision Warning (FCW), Traffic Jam Assist (TJA), Lane Departure Warning (LDW), and Lane Departure Prevention (LDP).
The MG HS PHEV also has Lane Keep Assistance (LKA) and Bling Spot Detection (BDS) features. It means the car is very safe for driving and travelling, making it a good choice for you and your family.

Price

As per MG Pakistan, the price of this new Hybrid Electric car is Rs8,499,000.
